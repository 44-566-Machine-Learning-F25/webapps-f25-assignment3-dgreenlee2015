Familiar Task - Washing Dishes

  1. Fill the sink with water.
  2. Add soap to water.
  3. Place dishes in water.
  4. Take washcloth and wash grime off of dishes.
  5. Rinse clean dish off.
  6. Place in drying rack.
  7. Put dishes away after dry.
